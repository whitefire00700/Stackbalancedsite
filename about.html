About Us

“You can’t make pizza with just cheese.”A single person can never create a difference. Irrespective of his skills and talent everyone needs a team to bring out the best in them. This is what caused the formation of this group. Individual talents found and complemented each other to form the team that is today. It all started when a freelance “content creator” decided to work with a freelance”web developer”. Right now you are probably thinking “Ok, so what’s so great about that?” . Yes it’s nothing great. They got together and completed the project that they had taken up. What is different in this relationship is that they decided to freelance “together”. They discovered that their performance in their respective fields was higher when they were a team. That is when the idea of freelancing as a group struck them. Over time they were joined by other like-minded people and this resulted in the formation of the freelance group “stackbalanced” . At stackbalanced we offer “web designing services” ,”search engine optimization services” ,”web app designing services” .Majority of our members are students are young energetic and raring to go. With no shortage of talent and bright minds (having people from different streams and different talents) we have expanded our services to app development and web programming We wish to expand our group and give an open invitation to all you geeks out there … come join us and Grow and learn more -Team WECN
